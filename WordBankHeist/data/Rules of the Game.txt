Rules of the Game:1. System will ask the player to enter a word with certain length and a starting character randomly generated.2. Length of the inputted word has to match along with the starting character to earn reward.3. Words cannot contain number, spaces, or special characters.4. Player cannot use same word more than once (System will generate Alert, if tried)5. Player starts with 3 lives or "RetryTokens", every time Player answer wrong, a RetryToken is consumed.6. Player earn a life after 5 consecutive right answers.7. Cash balance will be calculated based on length of character of correct word inputted.8. Once a word is used, and was an acceptable answer, you may not use the same word again.
